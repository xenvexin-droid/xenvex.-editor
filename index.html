What is Xenvex?

Xenvex is an online photo and video editing platform designed for creators. It allows users to edit, enhance, and customize content easily — directly from the browser, without installing heavy apps.

Features

Photo Editing

Crop, rotate, filters, text, and enhancements.

Video Editing

Trim, merge, speed control, and export.

Audio Support

Add background music and control volume.

Social Ratios

Instagram, YouTube & Shorts formats.
